In 2010, we opened the first nail salon where less is more. A thoughtfully designed space that's less about getting your nails "done" and more about caring for yourself and a world around you with formula that cleanse, nourish, color, and gloss, all without harmful ingredients. It was a beautiful beginning, one that led to additional spaces - each one unique.

When we sat out to create products that make it possible to bring Tenoverten salon experience home, non toxic was a necessity, and so were salon-quality results. Our formulas are not only "eight-free" (no dibutyl phtalate, toluene, formaldehyde, formaldehyde resin, camphor, ethyl tosylamide, xylene or triphenyl phosphate) but also cruelty free and vegan.
